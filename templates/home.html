RehabMate

RehabMate is an AI-powered physiotherapy assistant designed to make rehabilitation smarter, accessible, and more effective. Using advanced computer vision techniques, the system analyzes body movements in real time through a standard webcam, providing instant feedback on posture and exercise form. By transforming physical movements into measurable data like joint angles and range of motion, RehabMate enables accurate, objective, and consistent assessment without the need for expensive equipment. It empowers patients to perform exercises safely at home while supporting clinicians with data-driven insights for better treatment outcomes.
