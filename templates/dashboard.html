Squats

Lower body & core stability

Knee: 80-110 deg Hip: 70-110 deg
  1. 01 Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. 02 Keep your back straight and chest up.
  3. 03 Lower by bending knees and hips with control.
  4. 04 Push through heels to return to standing.

Hip Flexion

Hip mobility & muscle control

Hip: 130-140 deg Torso tilt: < 25 deg
  1. 01 Stand upright facing the camera.
  2. 02 Keep supporting leg straight.
  3. 03 Lift active leg to target angle, then lower slowly.
  4. 04 Follow real-time cues to improve form.

Knee Extension

Quadriceps strengthening

Extension: >145 deg Back upright
  1. 01 Sit on a chair with your back straight.
  2. 02 Extend knee until leg is straight.
  3. 03 Hold for about 2 seconds.
  4. 04 Return slowly to start position.

Hip Abduction (Standing)

Gluteus medius & lateral stability

Abduction: 25-50 deg Torso tilt: < 15 deg
  1. 01 Stand upright facing camera.
  2. 02 Keep torso straight and knee extended.
  3. 03 Lift one leg sideways to target range.
  4. 04 Lower slowly and repeat with control.

Hip Abduction (Sleeping)

Lateral hip strength

Abduction: 25-50 deg Back flat: < 15 deg
  1. 01 Lie on your side with both legs straight.
  2. 02 Keep your bottom leg still.
  3. 03 Lift your top leg slowly to target angle.
  4. 04 Lower with control and repeat.
